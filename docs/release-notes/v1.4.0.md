## RayMol 1.4.0

New Metal rendering effects and a redesigned settings panel.

### Rendering
- **Depth of field.** Photographic focus blur (bokeh) with adjustable focus distance, range, and aperture — *Scene ▸ Camera*.
- **Subsurface wrap.** Soft light-wrap past the terminator for a waxier, more lifelike surface — *Scene ▸ Lighting*.
- **Temporal ambient occlusion.** Ray-traced AO that accumulates to a clean result while the view is still — *Scene ▸ Shadows & AO* (needs Ray tracing).
- **Reduced-resolution upscale.** MetalFX upscaling to keep things smooth on slower GPUs — *Scene ▸ Quality*.

### Redesigned inspector panel
- Scene settings are reorganized into clear, collapsible groups — **Canvas, Camera, Lighting, Shadows & AO, Effects, Quality** — with dependent options tucked under their toggle and a **(?)** explainer on every control.
- **Scene, Objects, and Selections** are now consistent collapsible sections with aligned rows.

### Also
- Session save / ⌘S improvements.
- The macOS app is now fully self-contained (no external library dependencies).

Built on the open-source PyMOL engine. Updates install automatically via the in-app updater (**Check for Updates…** in the app menu).
