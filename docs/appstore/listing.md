**Description:**
RayMol brings high-quality molecular visualization to macOS, iPad, and iPhone —
built on the trusted PyMOL engine with a modern, native Metal renderer.

Load a structure from a file or fetch any entry from the Protein Data Bank, then
explore it with smooth, real-time graphics:

• Representations — cartoon, sticks, spheres, surface, mesh, ribbon, dots, and
  labels, with per-structure controls.
• Real-time quality — analytic ray-cast spheres and sticks, smooth tessellated
  cartoons, anti-aliasing, ambient occlusion, real-time shadows, and order-
  independent transparency.
• Hardware ray tracing — on Apple-silicon GPUs, add ray-traced ambient occlusion
  and shadows live, and export crisp high-resolution images.
• Themes — choose Classic, Paper, Sunset, or Dawn, or design your own with the
  live Theme Studio.
• Sequence, selections, and measurements — a synced sequence viewer, named
  selections, and interactive distance/angle/dihedral tools.
• Movies — build and export camera, scene, and state animations.
• Scripting — the full PyMOL command interface for power users.

RayMol runs entirely on your device. It collects no personal data and includes
no tracking or ads.
